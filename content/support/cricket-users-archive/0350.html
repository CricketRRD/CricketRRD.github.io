Take a look at the sample-config/atm-interfaces/Default file in the
Cricket distribution for the correct OIDs with an ATM interface on the
Cascade switch. I currently use the
sample-config/atm-interfaces/atm-trunk tree to monitor this same
critter.

It helps to have Cascade View to figure out the board/port interface
numbers.
