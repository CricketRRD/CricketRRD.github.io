From: Jeff Allen <jra@corp.webtv.net>

Brad Knowles wrote:
> Move everything down a subtree. Create your Defaults file at the
> "router" level, then within that directory, create a sub-directory

This is the right idea. listInterfaces is only meant to get you
started... most sites need to use sub directories to handle multiple
routers.

> Of course, you'll have to modify your cricket-data directory
> hierarchy to match, you'll have to recompile, and you'll probably
> have to start over with a fresh dataset.

As long as you correctly move the datafiles to their new homes, and as
long as you have not changed the target-types of the targets, then
there's no reason you need to lose your data.
