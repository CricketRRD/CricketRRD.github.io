Hi there,
   I have been spending days trying to track down the issues of
unreliability of the cricket application. After changing the
configuration, some interfaces quick working. Further investigation, I
found the error is almost always caused by line 890:
($mmax, undef, undef) = RRDs::graph @args;

Most people will see the error in the web server logs:
Premature end of scrip
t headers: /home/cricket/public_html/cricket/grapher.cgi

  After upgrading to the latest version of RRD, the issue is resolved.
I'd suggest people experiencing the same problem do the upgrade as well.

Good day.
