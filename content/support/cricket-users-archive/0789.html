Just a heads up, I have been running cricket on about 35 cisco routers for the
last few weeks and we have found that anytime a router gets rebooted it will
report hugely out of proportion information. For example, Router A's average
Bits In is 100kbits. Router A gets rebooted, for the next few minutes Bits In
jumps up to 4mbits. If your routers exibit this same behavior this would throw
off your "max bits" report. But if your goal is to watch for rebooted routers,
this might be handy. YMMV.
