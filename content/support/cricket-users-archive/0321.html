From: "Berg, Ivan" <iberg@montana.edu>

You should not have a problem under router-intefaces doing this. Just do a
router = 192.1.2.1 or something.

However, under routers(when querying cpu usage, temp, etc) try something
similar to this.

target router1
        router = 192.1.2.1
        snmp-host = %router%
        target-type = Cisco-7500-Router
