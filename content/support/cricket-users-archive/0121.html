Matthew Stier wrote:
> Some of the objects I would like to graph are percentages. How can
> I force the min/max ranges of a graph. (ie: min == 0, max ==100)

You need to put y-min and y-max in the "graph --default--" dictionary
for the subtree in question. See the http-performance subtree for an
example.

I just added this to the FAQ, since I've had this question before.
