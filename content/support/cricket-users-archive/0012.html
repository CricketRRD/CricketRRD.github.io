>I've got a problem here and would like to know whether anyone has
experienced it before and/or has come up with a solution.
>
>The thing is: my collector gathers up the data from the routers and

rrdtool dump.
>
>But sometimes, some of the graphs will only show zeroes (a flat horizontal
line covering the x axis). For instance, the hourly and weekly are zeroed
out and the daily and monthly are ok. Sometime later (after another sample
has been taken from the routers), everything is fine. Or even worse, some
of the graphs that were ok come up zeroed out and some come up fine. I see

zeroed graphs.

>What thrills me is that, even though it's intermitent, it's not a cacheing

of my browser's cache, and there's no proxy between me and the server machine.
>
>My setup:
>- Solaris 2.6 on a Sun Ultra 2,
>- Perl 5.0 patchlevel 4 subversion 4,
>- cricket-0.63,
>- rrdtool-0.99.22,
>- Digest-MD5-2.06,
>- SNMP_Session-0.70,
>- libwww-perl-5.42,
>- Time-HiRes-01.20,
>- HTML-Parser-2.22,
>- URI-1.02,
>- MIME-Base64-2.11,
>- libnet-1.0606.
>
>Do I miss something? I'm aware of rrdtool-0.99.25, but it's the above
