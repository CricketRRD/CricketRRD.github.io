From: rose@gerf.org

Check that the /tmp/cricket-cache directory is writeable by whatever
user runs apache on your server. This usually is the www user.

The CGI scripts are run as the www user, so they are the ones writing
the pictures to the /tmp/cricket-cache directory. Try chown'ing
the directory to the www user, and see if that solves your problem.

Cheers,
  Rose
