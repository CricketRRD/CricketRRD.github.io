Rob Shortt wrote:
> Is there a way to get Cricket to support SNMPv3?

Cricket will work with the SNMPv1 features of an SNMPv3
implementation. However, it does not yet support nifty things like
Counter64s, and certainly not spiffy authentication (did that make it
into SNMPv3?)

You should contact Simon Leinen <simon@switch.ch> to find out if
there's a project afoot to ad this to the SNMP implementation we are
using.
