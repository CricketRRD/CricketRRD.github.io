Phil,

I have a solution to our problem...revert to version 0.69 and all is
well.

I haven't had a chance to look at why this is yet, I intend to, but I'm
going to spend some time actually looking at the features of Cricket now
I have it working.

What I did was downloaded 0.69 from the Cricket home page, unzipped it,
untarred it, pointed my "cricket" link at the cricket-0.69 directory,
and ran "perl configure" to make sure all the scripts were looking in
the right place for my version of perl, I kept getting errors without
this last bit...

Hope this works for you...
