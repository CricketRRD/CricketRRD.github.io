From: "Utsav Ratti" <Utsav_Ratti@MCKINSEY.COM>

Here's a sample from my config:

target Whatever
        snmp-host = ip.address.goes.here
        target-type = Cisco-4500-Router

The key idea is that the target name can be pretty much whatever you want
it to be. The snmp-host tag/value pair will trigger a loopup using the IP
address.

Hope this helps.

Utsav
