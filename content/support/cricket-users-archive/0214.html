> I just upgraded from cricket 0.63 to 0.67 and noted that I can't have

Hmm. Actually, you were never really supposed to be able to have
them. Or at least, I didn't test it with that in mind.

You can go on using your patch if you want, or change things around so
that your target names do not need to have spaces. I plan to continue
NOT testing or supporting this, since it's not really a feature we
need -- and we do find it helpful to put comments on the lines of data
our exec programs make.
