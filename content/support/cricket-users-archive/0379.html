> I just had that problem. The Node.pm file is looking for
> ~cricket/config-tree. If you don't have the config-tree installed
> in the top level directory of a cricket user, you need to use
> the -base argument to compile:
>
> ./compile -base fullpath-to-config-tree
>
> I had to use:
>
> ./compile -base /home/redder/cricket-0.68/config-tree
>
