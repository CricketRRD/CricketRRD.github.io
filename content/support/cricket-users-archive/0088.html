From: Jeff Allen <jra@corp.webtv.net>

Sorry about the continuing saga, but there's some more things you need
to know about Cricket 0.64.

The "color" tag needs to have exactly two arguments. If the second
argument has spaces in it, then it must be surrounded in quotes. Thus,
this line, which used to be permissible:

                color --order-- dark-green, blue, red, cyan, magenta,
                     yellow, dark-red, dark-blue

now needs to have quoutes:

                color --order-- "dark-green, blue, red, cyan, magenta,
                     yellow, dark-red, dark-blue"

Separately, Cricket was not ignoring RCS files right, so I fixed that.

Finally, Cricket was colossally broken when dealing with config trees
more than one layer deep. (How did I manage to not test THAT!)

Thus, in about 5 minutes, I will release Cricket 0.65 will a bunch of
small fixes in it. Please don't continue to bang your head against
Cricket 0.64 -- wait, and bang your head against 0.65. (Kidding... I
hope.)
