From: "Berg, Ivan" <iberg@montana.edu>

Sounds like you don't have your the switch-ports listed under the items
to be collected. By default, collect-subtrees with normal option only
grabs stuff under the /router-intefaces config. Add the stuff you want
to monitor to the subtree-sets file. For instance use the following in
there:

set normal:
        /routers
        /router-interfaces
        /switch-ports
        /routing
