Hi Ulrik,

I was wondering if you had any luck with getting Cricket to work with
non-Cisco equipment. I'm trying to get Cricket to work with a Wellfleet
router, and the basic config works fine except for one small hitch.

I have one virtual device that actually comprises of 2 T1 lines. I can
monitor the aggregate traffic of the virtual device just fine, but I want
to monitor traffic of the individual T1 lines as well.

I obtained a Wellfleet MIB and used getif to browse the tree and find the
OIDs I want to monitor:

wfSyncRxOctets 1.3.6.1.4.1.18.3.4.5.1.36
wfSyncTxOctets 1.3.6.1.4.1.18.3.4.5.1.38

If I do an snmpwalk on each of these, I can see the 2 T1 lines (1.21 and
1.22 are appended to each OID). This gives me:

Line 1
   1.3.6.1.4.1.18.3.4.5.1.36.1.21 (Rx)
   1.3.6.1.4.1.18.3.4.5.1.38.1.21 (Tx)

Line 2
   1.3.6.1.4.1.18.3.4.5.1.36.1.22 (Rx)
   1.3.6.1.4.1.18.3.4.5.1.38.1.22 (Tx)

interfaces and be able to graph them?
