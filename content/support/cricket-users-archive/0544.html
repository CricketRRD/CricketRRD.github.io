Hi Dave,

I have got multi-targets working (i.e. multiple separate graphs) as
follows:

target INS_Peering_Points
                short-desc = "INS Peering Points - all routers"
                long-desc = "This page contains multiple graphs for INS
Peering Interfaces"
                targets = "/router-interfaces/routera/ethernet5_0;
                                /router-interfaces/routerb/ethernet2_1;
                        /router-interfaces/routerc/ethernet0;
                        /router-interfaces/routerd/ethernet0"

Hope that helps...
