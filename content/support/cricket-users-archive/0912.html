Try 'show-max = 1' in the graph dictionary. It's a little messy-looking
if you have more than one datasource already (this doubles the number of
datasources on the graph). I don't know of any way to graph ONLY the max
values, though it wouldn't be too difficult to implement. Look around
line 1367 in grapher.cgi (version 0.71).
