shawn@unifygroup.com wrote:
> Is there a binary distribution of cricket and rrdtool with perl
> modules that runs on NT ? I have compiled the rrdtool on NT but
> there is no instruction how to incorporate them into perl.

I am not aware of a binary distribution of RRD Tool. Cricket has no
required compiled components, so you should be able to download and
use it without a compiler, assuming you can find a copy of RRD Tool
that is precompiled.

You might talk to Philippe.Simonet@swisscom.com, who regularly sends
me patches to make Cricket work on NT. I do not develop or test it on
NT (yet).
