Ok, patch for cheap and cheerful 'deadhost' implementation, subject
to the caveats I spoke of before:

        <URL:http://www.gbnet.net/~jrg/cricket/snmpUtils.pm.diff>

Also, digging through the archives at onelist (dreadful interface) I
found someone's pointers to how to get the max-plotting working
correctly (the subject of a recent discussion)

Firstly, apply this patch (which also fixes a problem I had with failure.gif)
        <URL:http://www.gbnet.net/~jrg/cricket/grapher.cgi>

and then put the following in any graph section that you wish to plot
maximums for:

        graph <whatever>
                show-max = true
                max-color = <what-colour>

or it will pick the first colour and shift all the rest along if you
don't (but if you've hard coded any, anywhere, that's not good)

You don't have to alter the default in the code to get it to work, as
another poster implied.
