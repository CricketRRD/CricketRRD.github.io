Hi,

The targets and mtargets tags works on the target path, which doesn't
actually relate to the unix file names. You can have 10 targets in 1 file,
or 10 files of 1 target each. So, in my example, you can have a file
named interfaces that has Ethernet0_0 and Ethernet0_1, and then your
mtargets line could look like:

        mtargets="/router-interfaces/<routername>/Ethernet0_0;
                  /router-interfaces/<routername>/Ethernet0_1"

The filename never appears.

-Jeff
